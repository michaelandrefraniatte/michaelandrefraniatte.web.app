Listen Music on Your Computer

The program is made for listen all your music present on your computer well ordered in folders. It's simple to use and very intuituve. Click on the thumbnail to change music. Choose if you want to listen music of an artist or all artists. It loops through all your music at end of one. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

The program is simple to use and can be used on a simple pocket PC. There are a audio visualization of sounds and an equalizer for tweak it. The sound is the best sound you can ear from your MP3 files. The thumbnails are in JPG format, and must corresponds to the same names of your MP3 files. The MP3 files and thumbnails can be retrieved from the program NASH. You can download the sdk and program in release folder. There's a License.
