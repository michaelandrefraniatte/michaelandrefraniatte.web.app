Streamchat

Chat with Viewers of Your Stream

Optimize Your Streams

The program is a simple tool for optimize your stream on Twitch while playing a game. Instead of having a look on your stream, you can concentrate yourself on what you are streaming on Twitch, and the chat where viewers are posting messages. Your streaming capture and game you are playing are more optimized like that. Chat with viewers on your Twitch stream or simply have a look on it in an optimized way. The open folder button is made for open your channel chat with the file streamchat.txt containing your Twitch username. There is a purple circle indicating if your stream is on live, checked every 10 seconds. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

Streamchat can be used with Twitch Studio for streaming on Twitch easy and the better way because it's optimized. The file to open the chat shall contain only the name of the channel. You can try the web version of streamchat. You can download the sdk and program in release folder, to unzip. There's a License.
