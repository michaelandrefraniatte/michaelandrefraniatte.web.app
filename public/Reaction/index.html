Chemical Reaction Resolution

Program for Equilibration of Chemical Reactions

This program is for automatically equilibrate reactions with 3 to 9 constituents, and can be used in conjunction with EoSResol to synthetize molecules with 8 atomes. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

It can resolve every reactions in terms of number of products and reactants. It's nothing for I can making changes in derivative programs, or just one line text reaction program, because I made already the method to resolve every chemical reactions which are not to change in the program. You can download the sdk and program in release folder, to unzip. To note, a molecule shall be written with each type of atome grouped in number. For example, a carbone C shall appear only one time on a line. To note, the first product, under the first line just after the equal sign, shall have a stoechiometric coefficient set to 1.
