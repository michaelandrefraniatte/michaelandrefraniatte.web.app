GameplayCapture

Capture Your Gameplay

An Optimized Recorder for Your Gameplays

The program capture video and audio of your desktop and display your webcam to capture it also, if you have one, but no problem if you have no webcam, it will capture the rest. A 4% background border, around your webcam, can be set, with whatever your gif file, even with animation, if present. The numpad key dot, ., is used to start capture, the numpad key 0 is used to stop it, the numpad numbers are used to replace webcam around the screen, the numpad key 5 display the webcam in full screen on height axis and in center on width axis, the key multiply, *, is used to hide webcam, and the key substract, -, is used to change opacity, half to full. So, be ready, enable now your numpad ! Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

It can crash when the sound card used is from HDMI output audio, but simply the sound card can use a audio peripheral connected with a Wire Jack, to avoid crash. Also, sound shall be ear in the sound card chosen, to avoid frames be splited. To capture your webcam, the game you are playing, must be in windowed mode. If you don't see your webcam capture on the screen, while your game is in windowed mode, few switches between fullscreen and windowed mode with keys Alt + Enter or from game options, will display the capture. Of course, you have a back capture, for not losing any frames. You can close your webcam window, if you don't want to record it. For full optimized program, you need a animated GIF optimized for the border of webcam, it can be achieved with the web site ezgif.com. The webcam overlay is also available in a dedicated program. You can download the sdk and program in release folder.
