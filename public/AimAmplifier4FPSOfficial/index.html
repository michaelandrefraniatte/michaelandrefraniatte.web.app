AimAmplifier4FPS

Aim Ennemies Not Allies

Central Zone Zoomed as Overlay in FPS

This program is allowing to have a zoom of central zone as overlay in FPS games for competitive gamers. With it, you can play hardcore match without being kicked, because you killed an allies. You can recognize, easy peacy, ennemies between allies. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

The Numpad keys are allowing to relocate position of overlay, change the size, the transparency, and even make disapearing the overlay. It only run with windowed mode of your game, not fullscreen (toggle with Alt+Enter). You can download the sdk and program in release folder, to unzip.
