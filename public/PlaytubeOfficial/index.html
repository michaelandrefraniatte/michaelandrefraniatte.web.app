Playtube

Youtube Gallery Player

Keep Tracks of Your Best Videos You are Loving

The program Playtube is a Youtube gallery player where you add videos that can be embed. Make a great list of videos in Playtube for watching it, and share what you add with and in Playtube. Add all videos of a channel in 2 seconds. Or add one by one videos or mix playlists available on Youtube. Enjoy all the power of Youtube. The User Interface (UI) is user friendly for you can change colors as you want. There's nothing better to listen music with video clips of Artists. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

The folder of the program contains a colors text file for customize it. You can remove a video easy, because when you click on a picture, it copy in clipboard, the picture link containing the video id, and you can open a video in your favorite browser. You can add without any limits. You only need to accept Youtube RGPD. You can try the web version of playtube. I also made the Edge extension named playtube-extension.zip to unzip. The extension can be installed from edge://extensions/. It's made to use on top of Youtube like the program, but it don't allow to cut ads unlike the program. You can try my gallery by opening the example containing my playlists. Take care to always open the extension under same site because it use localstorage for saving the gallery. When you let a few times the cursor on buttons, you can see information bubbles, explaining the use of it.
